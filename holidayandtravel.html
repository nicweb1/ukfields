What are Holiday & Travel Claims

Holiday & Travel Compensation Claims

At UK Field Services, we know how disappointing it is when your holiday doesn’t go as planned. That’s why we help clients claim compensation and refunds for a wide range of travel-related issues. Here’s what we can assist with:

  • Cancelled or Disrupted Package Holidays:
    If your package holiday was cancelled or changed by the provider, you are entitled to a full refund or suitable alternative under the Package Travel Regulations. We’ll make sure you receive the refund or compensation you deserve for last-minute cancellations or major itinerary changes.
  • Accommodation & Hotel Complaints:
    We help with claims for hotels or resorts that were overbooked, not as described, unclean, unsafe, or lacking promised facilities. You have a right to compensation if your accommodation falls below reasonable standards.
  • Lost, Damaged or Delayed Luggage:
    If your luggage is lost, delayed, or damaged by a travel provider, cruise line, or tour operator (not just airlines), you can claim for the value of lost items, emergency essentials, and inconvenience. We guide you through the claims process under travel law and the Montreal Convention.
  • Excursion and Activity Failures:
    If paid excursions, tours, or activities were cancelled, unsafe, or not delivered as promised, you could be due a refund or compensation—whether it’s for missed theme parks, day trips, or unsafe adventure activities.
  • Hidden Charges & Unfair Fees:
    If you were hit with surprise charges or resort fees not disclosed upfront, we’ll help you recover these costs.
  • Overbooking & Alternative Accommodation:
    Arrived to find your hotel overbooked and moved to a lesser property? We claim for loss of value, inconvenience, and additional costs.
  • Tour Operator or Provider Collapse:
    If your provider or tour operator went out of business, we assist with ATOL claims or credit card chargeback for your losses.
  • Travel Disruptions (Coach, Ferry, Rail):
    We help claim for non-flight delays or cancellations—coach, ferry, or train—so you get compensated for disruption, extra expenses, or missed connections.
  • Timeshare Mis-selling & Holiday Clubs:
    If you were mis-sold a timeshare, pressured into joining a holiday club, or locked into unfair contracts, we can help you claim compensation or exit your contract. This includes situations where the property was misrepresented, ongoing fees are excessive, or you were promised benefits that never materialised. UK Field Services specialises in reclaiming money lost on mis-sold timeshare agreements and unlawful holiday clubs, in line with UK and EU consumer protection laws.

With UK Field Services, you won’t need to handle complex paperwork or difficult negotiations with travel companies. Our team manages every step of the claim—collecting evidence, preparing documentation, and negotiating for your best outcome. Let us help you secure the compensation or refund you’re entitled to, so you can move forward with peace of mind.
