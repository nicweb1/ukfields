What can we offer with insurance claims

Insurance Claims for Consumers & Businesses

Insurance is meant to protect you—but when a claim is denied, delayed, or underpaid, it can feel like the odds are stacked against you. UK Field Services supports both individuals and businesses in challenging insurance companies and recovering the compensation you’re rightfully owed. We manage every stage of the process, from paperwork to negotiation and escalation, so you don’t have to.

Insurance Claims We Can Assist With:

  • Home & Contents Insurance: Claims for theft, fire, flooding, leaks, accidental damage, or subsidence—especially if the insurer disputes liability or undervalues your loss.
  • Motor & Vehicle Insurance: Disputes over vehicle write-offs, repairs, non-fault accidents, hire car cover, or non-payment of claims.
  • Travel Insurance: Cancelled trips, medical emergencies abroad, lost luggage, missed connections, or airline failure—claims for both consumers and business travelers.
  • Business Insurance: Business interruption, property damage, professional indemnity, public/product liability, cyber insurance, and employer’s liability claims.
  • Health & Life Insurance: Denied or delayed payments for critical illness, income protection, life insurance, or private medical claims.
  • Pet Insurance: Claims refused for vet bills, surgery, or ongoing treatments.
  • Gadget & Mobile Insurance: Broken, lost, or stolen devices where the insurer delays, disputes, or refuses the claim.
  • Landlord & Rental Property Insurance: Claims for property damage, unpaid rent, or legal costs—support for landlords and property managers.
  • Income Protection & Payment Protection Insurance (PPI): Issues with denied payouts or unfair policy exclusions.
  • Group & Commercial Schemes: Claims under group policies for staff, fleet, or collective risk.
  • Unfair Rejection or Delay: Insurers dragging out investigations, using technicalities to avoid paying, or offering low settlements—we challenge decisions and push for what you’re owed.
  • Ombudsman Complaints & Legal Escalation: If the insurer won’t budge, we’ll help escalate your case to the Financial Ombudsman Service or introduce you to a specialist solicitor for legal action—always with your consent.

Our Insurance Claims Process

  1. Free Claim Review: Tell us about your insurance problem. We assess your case, check your policy, and explain your options.
  2. Evidence & Documentation: We help you gather the right documents—photos, reports, receipts, or correspondence—to support your claim.
  3. Claim Submission & Negotiation: Our experts draft strong letters, challenge unfair refusals, and negotiate with insurers to get the maximum settlement.
  4. Escalation if Needed: If the insurer still refuses, we prepare a formal complaint to the ombudsman or, if needed, connect you with specialist legal partners.
  5. Resolution & Payment: We keep you informed every step of the way, ensuring your claim is paid out and your rights are respected.

Don’t let insurance companies give you the runaround—let UK Field Services handle the hassle, and secure the compensation you deserve.
